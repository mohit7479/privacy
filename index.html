Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Counter108 (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

1. About Counter108

Counter108 is a simple counter app that allows users to increase or decrease a numeric value using on-screen buttons. It is designed to work fully offline and does not require any sign up or login.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Photos, media, or files from your device (other than what the operating system itself manages)
  • Any usage analytics or tracking identifiers (unless you later add such services and update this policy)

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store small pieces of information locally on your device, for example:

  • Your current counter value
  • Any app preferences (such as theme, sound, or vibration settings, if available)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party by the App.

4. Third-Party Services

At the time of this Privacy Policy, Counter108 does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If we add any third-party services in the future (for example, analytics or ads), we will update this Privacy Policy to describe:

  • What services are used
  • What data they may collect
  • How you can control or opt out where applicable

5. Children’s Privacy

Counter108 is a simple utility app and does not target children specifically. Since we do not collect any personal data from users, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under the age defined by local laws).

6. Security

Because the App does not send your data to our servers, the main protection for your data is your device itself. We recommend that you:

  • Use a secure lock screen (PIN, password, fingerprint, etc.)
  • Keep your operating system and apps up to date

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
